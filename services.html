02

DISC FOR
DOCTORS & TEAMS

Know how to talk to anyone. Convert more patients because you understand how they think.

Not every prospect communicates the same way — and if your team is using the same script on every caller, they're leaving money on the table. The DISC Model of Human Behavior gives your team the ability to identify a prospect's communication style and adapt on the fly.

Identify. Adapt. Convert. When you learn to communicate on your prospect's level — not yours — the "hard sell" disappears. Patients feel understood, not pressured.

Instantly identify a patient's communication style from the first 60 seconds
Adapt language, pace, and approach to match their decision-making style
Reduce friction in consultations and follow-up conversations
Improve team communication and internal culture
Learn More →
THE FOUR STYLES
D Dominant Results-focused, direct, decisive. Hates small talk.
I Influential People-focused, optimistic, enthusiastic. Buys on feeling.
S Steady Relationship-focused, patient, loyal. Needs reassurance.
C Conscientious Data-focused, analytical, cautious. Needs the facts.

Cataract Training
03

CATARACT
CONVERSION
MASTERCLASS

Turn Your Cataract Pipeline Into More Premium Conversions

The biggest revenue opportunity in your practice is hiding in plain sight — and it's on your cataract evaluation schedule. You already have the patients coming in. They already need the procedure. The question is: are you maximizing their visual outcomes AND your revenue per case?

The cataract evaluation is a once-in-a-lifetime conversation about what the patient wants their vision to look like for the next 20-30 years. Your team needs to confidently lead that conversation — from "What does insurance cover?" to "What do YOU want to see?"

Start with vision goals (not insurance limits). Present premium as the solution (not an upsell). Guide patients to the right decision.

When you do this well, Advanced Technology package conversions become natural — and your practice captures the revenue that's already walking through your door.

Lead with vision goals, not insurance limits
Position premium lenses as the solution to their unique visual needs
Navigate the insurance conversation with confidence
Convert more evaluations to Advanced Technology packages
